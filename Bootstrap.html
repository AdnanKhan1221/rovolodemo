Education is a systematic process of learning something with the goal of acquiring knowledge. It can help you learn facts, concepts, and theories. Education is a broader concept than training. Education prepares people for a career by teaching them the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their chosen field. Education can be done in a classroom or any other environment. Training is the process of learning something with a goal of performing a specific skill or behavior. Training prepares people for a specific job by teaching them the skills and knowledge they need to do that job.
